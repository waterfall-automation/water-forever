JALNA PILOT
Jalna
01 · Grounding The Vision

Why Jalna District?

Reservoirs, rural drinking water networks, and agricultural irrigation are typically managed as isolated silos. Jalna provides the ideal operational testing ground to bridge these silos into a single, connected intelligence layer.

CWPRS VALIDATED

1. Reservoir Intelligence

Digitising storage conditions to eliminate blind spots in surface water availability.

  • Real-time reservoir storage estimation
  • Inflow, outflow & evaporation analysis
  • Water availability forecasting
  • Early drought & flood indicator alerts
RAJEGAON PILOT

2. Rural Water Distribution

Grounding the framework in the ongoing Rajegaon Rural Water Supply Pilot.

  • Village water withdrawal monitoring
  • Distribution performance & leakage tracking
  • Transparent village-level allocation
  • Operational automation & service reliability
FIELD PROVEN

3. Agricultural Intelligence

Tracking the largest water withdrawal sector to balance agricultural & domestic demand.

  • Irrigation pump cluster monitoring
  • Crop water demand estimation
  • Community irrigation analytics
  • Water-use efficiency insights

The Principle of True Reservoir Intelligence

"Reservoir conditions, rural water supply withdrawals, and agricultural water withdrawals must be viewed together. Only when every major withdrawal is connected can decision-makers gain a complete understanding of water availability, demand, and long-term sustainability."

ROADMAP
Phases
03 · Phased Strategy

Implementation Roadmap

Rather than attempting district-wide digitisation from the outset, the Jalna POV establishes one complete reservoir-scale demonstration where every major withdrawal is connected. This model is then progressively replicated.

PHASE 01 Under Progress

Digital Rural Water Distribution

Deploying digital monitoring across the ongoing Rajegaon Rural Water Supply Pilot to establish village-level withdrawal transparency.

Rajegaon Pilot Groundwork
PHASE 02 Next Step

Reservoir Intelligence Demonstration

Deploying CWPRS-validated technology at Mandala Talab to estimate storage, monitor water balance, and forecast sustainability.

CWPRS-Validated Storage
PHASE 03 Next Step

Agricultural Water Intelligence

Digitising one community irrigation cluster (50–100 agricultural pumps) to quantify agricultural demand alongside reservoir storage.

50–100 Pump Digitisation
PHASE 04 Future Step

Connected Reservoir Ecosystem

Scaling across all agricultural pump clusters (~385 pumps) and rural water supply points to unify Mandala Talab into one connected ecosystem.

Full Reservoir Unification
PHASE 05 Future Step

District Replication

Replicating the validated single-reservoir framework across additional reservoirs to build Jalna's district-scale Digital Water Infrastructure.

District-Wide Scale
